Field Notes & Flags

Insurance Coordination: Customer has an active Allstate claim for roof/dormer damage. Primary leak source identified as a loose or dismantled shingle near dormer edge above the bow window. Water suspected to be tracking down rafters into the T&G pine ceiling. Scope may be partially covered — coordinate with customer re: adjuster involvement before finalizing quote.
Log Home Interior — Critical Consideration: This is a full log home with exposed round log walls and knotty pine T&G ceiling. All interior trim, jamb extensions, and replacement ceiling boards must match existing materials, stain, and profile. Standard install methods do not apply — allow additional time for fit and finish.
Height Sizing: New Pella unit (51.5" as spec'd) must be verified against actual buck opening before ordering. Reduce height slightly as needed to ensure clean fitment without modifying the existing log buck frame.
Concealed Conditions: Extent of rot and moisture damage unknown until window is removed. Formal quote to include concealed conditions language per M&P T&C — any additional rot remediation, structural repairs, or mold treatment will be documented and change-ordered.
Containment: T&G ceiling demo requires full poly containment to protect log home interior. Dust and debris management is critical given finished surfaces.
