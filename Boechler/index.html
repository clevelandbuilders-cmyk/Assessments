Project Summary

We'll be removing the existing vinyl siding on the east-facing garage wall, inspecting and wrapping the sheathing as needed, then installing LP SmartSide lap siding with coordinating trim boards and soffit/fascia. All product delivered primed only — Travis and Brittany will paint to their preferred color after installation is complete.

Important — Customer Responsibility

All LP SmartSide siding and trim will be delivered and installed primed only. Travis and Brittany are responsible for painting all surfaces after installation. Paint selection and application is not included in this contract.

Sheathing & WRB — TBD at Demo

The condition of the wall sheathing is unknown until existing vinyl is removed. House wrap will be installed or repaired as needed. Any sheathing repairs will be scoped and priced separately.

Scope Boundary

This assessment covers the east-facing garage wall only. No other elevations, windows, doors, or interior work are included. Any additional work requires a separate assessment and quote.
