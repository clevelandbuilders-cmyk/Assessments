Exterior
LP SmartSide Lap Siding & Trim — Full Re-Side, All Four Elevations

The existing vinyl lap siding will be fully removed down to the sheathing across the house and garage. We'll install new LP SmartSide 8" lap siding with a 7" exposure, along with new 1-1/4" LP SmartSide trim boards at all corners, window and door casings, and gable rakes. This gives the home a clean, durable, paintable exterior with a more substantial, modern look than vinyl.

Full removal of existing vinyl lap siding — house and garage
New LP SmartSide 8" lap siding, 16' lengths, ~7" exposure
New 1-1/4" LP SmartSide trim boards, 16' lengths, at corners, window/door casings, and gable rakes
Coverage across all elevations — East, South, West, North — plus garage
