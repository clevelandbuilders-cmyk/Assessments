Project Overview

This assessment covers the full-frame replacement of four openings at the Schultz residence on Milwaukee's Lower East Side: three French door units on the front elevation and one egress casement window in the finished basement, along with two smaller service repairs.

All four replacement units are Pella® Lifestyle Series wood products with Hartford Green aluminum-clad exteriors and Bright White painted interiors — a combination that preserves the home's historic character on the street side while delivering a clean, modern interior finish.

Full-frame replacement means each existing unit is removed down to the rough opening — frame, jambs, and trim — and the new unit is installed, insulated, flashed, and trimmed as new construction. This approach addresses any hidden deterioration in the existing frames and ensures the new units perform to their full potential.

Why full frame? Insert-style replacements reuse the old frame and inherit its problems. On openings of this age and exposure, full-frame replacement is the right call — it's more work, but it's done once and done right.
